Update AdGuard Home manually

If the automatic AdGuard Home update fails In this post, ‘Update AdGuard Home manually’, and the accompanying video tutorial, I will show you how to perform a manual update on an AdGuard Home instance. Why update AdGuard Home manually? Some AdGuard Home updates fail to update automatically, for example, version “AdGuard Home v0.107.17.” Therefore, it is necessary to perform a manual update via the command line.
